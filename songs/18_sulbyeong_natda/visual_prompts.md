> **톤: tragicomic (짠함 + 우스움 동시).** 사실적 사진 톤이되 자세·소품·분위기는 엽기·개그·짠함이 섞임.

## 캐릭터 기준 컷 (Reference Portrait)

> 다른 컷 생성 시 이 이미지를 베이스로 쓸 수 있는 표준 캐릭터 이미지.

### 영문 프롬프트 (메인)

```
A photorealistic, cinematic medium-shot portrait of a young Korean man
in his early 30s — a tragicomic 90s-era 회식(after-work drinking) cliché character.

He wears a 1990s-style oversized boxy navy or charcoal suit, completely disheveled:
jacket open and crumpled, white shirt half-untucked with two buttons missing,
loosely knotted tie crooked or hanging like a noose, top button broken.
Light 3-day stubble on his face, slightly tousled black hair sticking up in places,
red flushed nose and cheeks from drinking, eyes glassy and unfocused,
a single absurd detail (one suspender slipped off his shoulder, OR a small
piece of food stuck to his collar, OR his glasses crooked on his face).

Body faces forward toward the camera, but the head is turned to the side
in a 3/4 or near-profile view — only a sliver of his face visible.
He occupies roughly one-third (33%) of the frame, with generous negative
space around him so typography can be placed in the upper-right or left.

His expression mixes deep weariness with an absurd half-smile —
the look of a man who knows he is pathetic and finds it funny.
Tragicomic, NOT pure tragedy and NOT pure comedy — both at once.

Late-night ambient lighting, low-contrast desaturated palette,
warm amber neon highlights against cool blue shadows, evoking 1990s Seoul
late-night bar / 포장마차 atmosphere. Shot on 35mm film, fine grain,
shallow depth of field, background softly out of focus.

Style: cinematic still in the spirit of Roy Andersson's deadpan
Scandinavian tragicomedy meets 1990s Korean late-night TV drama meets
smoky jazz album cover. Photorealistic, NOT cartoonish, NOT anime,
NOT illustration — but the staging itself is darkly funny.
```

## 변형 컷 (트랙·시퀀스별)

### Part I — 빈병 (트랙 1·2·3)

**1. 빈 술상 옆에 졸기 직전 (트랙 1 "잔이 비었다")**

```
Same character, sitting alone at a small wooden 포장마차 table,
slumped forward, head propped on one hand which is sliding off,
about to face-plant into the empty soju shot glass. Side profile view.
Empty 참이슬 bottle tipped on its side, one peanut on the floor.
He occupies left third of frame, generous negative space on the right
showing softly blurred late-night street lamps. Single warm pendant light overhead.
Tragicomic — about to fall asleep on the table, doesn't know it.
Photorealistic 35mm film grain, shallow depth of field, deadpan staging.
```

### Part II — 몸병 (트랙 4·5·6)

**2. 머리에 넥타이 클라이맥스 (트랙 6) ⭐**

```
Same character, in full Korean 회식 cliché peak: he has wrapped his necktie
around his head like a Rambo headband, suit jacket dragged off one shoulder,
shirt 3 buttons open. He's holding a half-empty 참이슬 bottle in one hand
like a microphone, mouth open mid-(silent) song, eyes closed in
self-mocking ecstasy. Standing in an empty Seoul alleyway at 2 AM,
neon convenience store sign glowing behind him.
Side or 3/4 angle. He occupies right third, left side shows empty
neon-lit alley out of focus. Tragicomic peak — ridiculous AND heartbreaking.
Photorealistic, deadpan composition (no exaggerated cartoon expressions),
35mm film grain, low-contrast warm tones with green-blue neon accents.
```

**3. 새벽 세 시 편의점 앞 (트랙 5)**

```
Same character, drunken collapse stage. Sitting on the curb outside
a 24-hour convenience store at early dawn, cradling an empty cup-noodle
container like a baby, one shoe off (stocking foot visible), suit jacket
inside-out somehow. His head is tilted up at the sky as if asking the
universe a question, single tear about to fall — but mouth slightly
turned up in a confused smile. Empty plastic bag beside him, a
crumpled receipt blowing away. Streetlamp warm pool of light on him,
deep blue darkness beyond. He occupies center-bottom third,
upper two-thirds is empty cold dark sky. 35mm film, photorealistic, somber-funny.
```

### Part III — 회복 (트랙 7·8·9)

**4. 해장국에 머리 박기 직전 (트랙 7)**

```
Same character, the morning after, slightly less disheveled but still rough.
Sitting at a small Korean diner counter, a steaming bowl of haejangguk
in front of him. He's leaning forward, face hovering 5cm above the soup,
eyes half-closed — about to either drop his face into it or surrender
and eat. Side profile, gentle morning light from window.
Occupies left third of frame, right side shows soft empty diner background
with another silent diner reading newspaper out of focus.
Tragicomic recovery — barely surviving but trying. Photorealistic,
warm daylight tones — first warmth in the album sequence, deadpan staging.
```

**5. 술병이 다 나았다 — 거울 앞 자기 객관화 (트랙 9 졸업)**

```
Same character, fully composed now, suit clean and pressed, hair combed,
shaved. Standing in front of a small bathroom mirror, looking at his own
reflection with a small absurd half-smile — the look of "I survived myself."
3/4 angle showing his back and the reflected face, OR side profile from
behind him. He occupies right third of frame, left side shows empty
soft window light or empty white tile. The bathroom shelf has ONE
folded paper boat made out of a soju bottle label — small absurd detail
hinting at what he went through. Photorealistic, gentle warm daylight,
quiet resolution. Deadpan, NOT triumphant, NOT smug — just quietly amused.
```

## 사용 메모

- **모델 추천**: GPT-4 / DALL-E 3, Grok, Midjourney v6+
- **반복 생성 시**: 첫 번째 결과를 reference로 첨부하면 캐릭터 일관성 ↑
- **금지 키워드**: anime, cartoon, illustration, k-pop idol, glamorous,
  perfect-skin, *exaggerated cartoon expression*, *over-the-top slapstick*
  (사실적 사진 톤은 유지)
- **강조 키워드**: photorealistic, 35mm film grain, 1990s Korea,
  smoky jazz, low-contrast, *tragicomic*, *deadpan staging*,
  *Roy Andersson*, *absurd small detail*
- **여백 33% 컨셉**: 캐릭터가 작게 들어가고 주변 여백 많이 — 텍스트(곡 제목/앨범 제목) 얹을 공간
- **개그·엽기 강도 조절**: "absurd small detail one item" 가이드로 *한 컷에 한 가지 어이없는 디테일*만.
  너무 많이 쌓으면 코미디 과해서 정서 깨짐. 적게 쓰는 게 더 짠함.

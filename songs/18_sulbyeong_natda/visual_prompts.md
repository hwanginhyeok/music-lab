## 캐릭터 기준 컷 (Reference Portrait)

> 다른 컷 생성 시 이 이미지를 베이스로 쓸 수 있는 표준 캐릭터 이미지.

### 영문 프롬프트 (메인)

```
A photorealistic, cinematic medium-shot portrait of a young Korean man in his early 30s.
He wears a 1990s-style oversized boxy single-breasted suit, navy or charcoal,
with wide lapels and broad shoulder pads, a slightly wrinkled white shirt,
and a loosely knotted dark tie. Light 3-day stubble on his face,
slightly tousled black hair, tired but quietly composed expression.

Body faces forward toward the camera, but the head is turned to the side
in a 3/4 or near-profile view — only a sliver of his face is visible.
He occupies roughly one-third (33%) of the frame, with generous negative
space around him so typography can be placed in the upper-right or left.

Late-night ambient lighting, low-contrast desaturated palette,
warm amber highlights against cool blue shadows, evoking 1990s Seoul
late-night bar atmosphere. Shot on 35mm film, fine grain, shallow depth
of field, background softly out of focus.

Style: cinematic still, somewhere between Wong Kar-wai's mood and
a smoky jazz album cover from the 1960s. Realistic, not stylized,
not anime, not illustration.
```

## 변형 컷 (트랙·시퀀스별)

### Part I — 빈병 (트랙 1·2·3)

**1. 빈 술상 앞 옆모습** (트랙 1 "잔이 비었다")

```
Same character (90s navy suit, light stubble, young Korean man),
sitting alone at a small wooden bar table, side profile view,
empty soju bottle and one empty shot glass on the table.
He occupies left third of frame, generous negative space on the right
showing a softly blurred bar interior. Late-night ambient lighting,
single warm pendant light overhead, smoky atmosphere.
Photorealistic 35mm film grain, shallow depth of field.
He is not looking at the bottle, gaze drifting somewhere off-frame.
```

### Part II — 몸병 (트랙 4·5·6)

**2. 머리에 넥타이 (캐릭터 핵심 — 트랙 6)**

```
Same character, but disheveled — suit jacket open, white shirt half-untucked,
his necktie wrapped loosely around his head like a 90s Korean
post-회식 cliché, holding a half-empty soju bottle (참이슬).
Side or 3/4 profile, slight smile of self-mockery on his face.
He occupies right third of frame, left side empty showing late-night
empty Seoul alleyway with neon glow softly out of focus.
Tragicomic mood — pity and humor in one frame.
Photorealistic, cinematic, NOT cartoonish.
35mm film grain, low-contrast warm tones with green-blue neon accents.
```

**3. 새벽 세 시 (트랙 5)**

```
Same character, drunken low point. Sitting on a curb outside a convenience store
in the early morning hours, head tilted down or turned away. Empty plastic bag
beside him. Streetlamp casts a single warm pool of light on him,
everything else fading to deep blue darkness. He occupies center-bottom third,
upper two-thirds is empty cold dark sky. 35mm film, photorealistic, somber.
```

### Part III — 회복 (트랙 7·8·9)

**4. 해장국 (트랙 7)**

```
Same character, shaved cleaner now, wearing a softer/lighter version of
the same 90s suit (jacket off, shirt sleeves rolled up). Sitting at a small
Korean diner table with a steaming bowl of haejangguk in front of him.
Side profile, gentle morning light coming through a window from his right.
Occupies left third, right side shows soft empty diner background.
Slight relief on his face, NOT smiling, just quiet survival.
Photorealistic, warm daylight tones — first warmth in the album sequence.
```

**5. 술병이 다 나았다 (트랙 9 — 졸업)**

```
Same character, fully composed now, suit clean and pressed, no bottle in hand.

Soft afternoon light, calm composure. Occupies right third of frame,
left side shows empty soft window light. Photorealistic, gentle warm tones,
quiet resolution. The room behind him is empty of bottles.
```

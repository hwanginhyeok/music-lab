# GPT 이미지 생성 프롬프트

## 영문 프롬프트

A minimalist watercolor illustration in soft Japanese / Korean storybook style.

Scene: Pure white snowy background, slightly textured paper feel.
A small child stands alone in the center, facing forward with a quiet, restrained expression.
Tears slowly slide down their cheek — one or two drops, with a barely visible cool blue tint.
Snowflakes drift gently across the frame.

Style references: Studio Ghibli soft hand-drawn lines, Jon Klassen muted palette, Beatrice Alemagna emotional minimalism. Trembling pencil/ink strokes, almost monochrome (white, soft grey, hint of cool blue only on tears).

Mood: Contained sorrow, longing, the silence of falling snow. The kind of sadness that sits down quietly beside you, never raising its voice.

Generate two versions of this same scene:

- Version A (t=0): Snow has just begun to fall. Almost no snow on the child's head. One or two tears just starting to fall.

- Version B (t=+1 second): Same composition, same expression, but a thin layer of snow has settled on the child's hair and shoulders, like a soft veil. One more tear may have fallen. Time has visibly passed — but nothing else has moved.

Aspect ratio: 16:9.
No text in the image.
The two versions must be visually identical in pose, framing, and expression — only the accumulated snow changes, making the passage of time the only visible difference.

## 사용 안내
- ChatGPT (DALL-E) / Midjourney / Sora 등 멀티모달 이미지 생성 도구에 위 프롬프트 그대로 입력
- 두 장 받아서 thumbnail/cover_a.jpg (t=0) + thumbnail/cover_b.jpg (t=+1s) 저장
- YouTube 썸네일은 cover_b 사용 (시간 흐른 모습이 더 정서적)
- 영상 인트로/아웃트로 모션은 cover_a → cover_b 페이드 활용 가능
